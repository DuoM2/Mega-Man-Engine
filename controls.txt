--- In-game Controls ---

WASD - Move
/    - Jump
.    - Shoot
,    - Slide  (or using [D + /] will also do the trick)
Q    - Switch Weapon Left
E    - Switch Weapon Right


--- UI Controls ---

For UI control, you may need to use arrow keys and Enter button instead of the default keys provided above.

Arrow Keys - Move
Enter      - Accept
Space      - Accept
Esc        - Cancel
F1-F4      - Change the screen size to corresponding F-number
F5         - Toggle Fullscreen on/off
